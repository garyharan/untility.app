Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

🔒 Privacy Summary

Untility is designed with your privacy in mind. All

your event data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or data on external servers. Your countdown events stay on your device, private and secure.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Untility ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in relation to the Untility mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Don't Collect

Untility does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Event data or countdown information you create
  • Photos or other media
  • Contact information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How Your Data is Stored

All information you create within Untility, including:

  • Event names and dates
  • Countdown configurations
  • Theme preferences
  • Widget settings

is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers, third parties, or cloud services operated by us.

Third-Party Services

Untility does not integrate

with, use, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device and we don't collect or transmit any information, your data security is protected by your device's own security features, including:

  • Device passcode/biometric authentication
  • iOS sandboxing and security features
  • Your iCloud backup settings (if enabled)

We recommend keeping your device secure and up-to-date with the latest iOS security updates.

Children's Privacy

Untility does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App can be safely used by users of all ages.

iCloud and Device Backups

If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your locally stored Untility data may be included in your device backups according to Apple's standard backup policies. This is controlled entirely by your device settings and Apple's privacy practices, not by Untility.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any of your data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your data through the App's interface and your device settings. You can delete the App at any time to remove all associated data from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to how we handle user information (for example, if we start collecting data in the future), we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy and, where appropriate, providing notice through the App or App Store.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Since Untility does not collect any personal information, many provisions of these regulations do not apply to our App.
